\medium
The number preceding the name of the star (or binary or multiple system of stars) refers to its approximate rank 
according to apparent magnitude.  ``RA'' stands for ``Right Ascension'' and ``Decl.'' for ``Declination''.
A ``v'' following the apparent or absolute magnitude of a star (or system of stars) means that this value varies.

The sections are designated as two numbers separated by a slash.  The first number refers to the spherical biangle
sections and the second to the panel sections.
